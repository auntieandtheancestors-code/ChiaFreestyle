THE 6-WEEK TRANSFORMATION

This is not a quick fix. This is a six-week online program built to genuinely change how you move, perform, and feel in your body — through a combination of structured training, mobility work, and real sport nutrition coaching.

The program is a collaboration between Chia and sport nutrition coach Michael Cittadini, who found his path through personal training and brings deep expertise in fuelling movement and body transformation from the inside out.

Together they have designed a program that works as a whole — so your training, your mobility, and what you eat all support each other. Everything is delivered online, so you can follow it from anywhere in the world.
